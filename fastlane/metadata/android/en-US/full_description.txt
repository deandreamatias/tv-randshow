<p><i>TV Randshow</i> was created to help choose a random episode from your favorites TV shows. The TV shows database comes from the TMDB and the app saves them in a database on the device. With the list of favorites, you can roll the dice of a TV shows to get a random episode.</p><p><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>Save your favorites TV shows</li><li>Choose a random episode from a favorite TV show</li></ul>
